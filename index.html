Vedic Astrologer  ·  Vastu Consultant  ·  Toronto, Canada

Namaste! I am a Toronto-based Vedic consultant with a deep-rooted passion for the ancient science of Jyotish. What sets my practice apart is my approach — I do not rely on one system alone. By carefully comparing and cross-referencing Vedic Astrology with Western Progressions, I aim to deliver readings of the highest accuracy — consistently striving for results you can truly rely on.

I believe astrology is far more than prediction. It is a sacred tool for self-discovery. Through Past Life Astrology, I help you understand the deeper purpose of your birth — the karmic imprints you carry, the lessons your soul has chosen, and the direction your life is truly meant to take. When you understand why you are here, every decision becomes clearer.

Beyond the stars, I am a devoted practitioner of Sanskrit and Classical Music — proudly sharing the heritage of Vedic Slokas, Telugu language and the Violin with the next generation.
